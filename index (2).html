Final Year Project · 2025–2026
Autonomous Mobile Platform for Melon-Harvesting Robotic Arm

Designed and built a differential-drive autonomous vehicle to carry a robotic arm for melon harvesting. Implemented closed-loop PID control using IMU (MPU6050) feedback for stability, and integrated depth camera (Orbbec) for real-time fruit detection and distance estimation. Performed structural load analysis to ensure platform stability under robotic arm loading.

NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano Raspberry Pi 4B PID Control IMU MPU6050 BTS7960 Motor Driver RGB-Depth Camera Python
KEY RESULTS
▸ PID stable within ±2° deadzone threshold
▸ Emergency stop at Roll > 21°, Pitch > 28°
▸ Platform stable under 7 kg with 0.6 m arm reach
▸ IMU polling at 50 Hz real-time
